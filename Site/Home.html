How it works

Download the EcoRide app. Fill out the required information. Users will need to register their driver's license to start using our vehicles.

Find your nearest car and reserve it! You can choose a free reservation time from 15 to 25 minutes.

When you arrive at the reserved vehicle, click "Start Trip".

To complete the rental, park the car in an approved location. Exit, close the doors, and press "End Trip".
